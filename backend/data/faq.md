# FAQ

## What information is required before recommending a PLC?

Please confirm I/O points, power supply, output type, communication interface, protocol, application, and quantity.

## What information is required before selecting a VFD?

Please confirm motor power, input voltage, phase, motor type, application, communication requirement, and quantity.

## What information is needed for HMI selection?

Please confirm screen size, resolution, communication interface, protocol, power supply, PLC compatibility, and quantity.

## How to choose an industrial switch?

Please confirm port count, speed, PoE requirement, managed or unmanaged type, fiber port requirement, power input, and working temperature.

## Can the Agent quote price automatically?

In the initial version, the Agent does not quote price. Price, stock, and lead time must be confirmed manually by sales staff.

## Can the Agent promise delivery time?

No. Delivery time should be confirmed manually because it depends on inventory and supply chain status.

## What does match score mean?

Match score estimates how well a candidate product matches extracted customer requirements based on category, technical parameters, and keywords.

## What happens when information is missing?

The Agent generates clarification questions instead of forcing a final recommendation.
